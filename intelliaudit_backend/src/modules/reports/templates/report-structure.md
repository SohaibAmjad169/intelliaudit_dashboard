# Report Template Structure

Based on the Word document, here's the structure of the full report:

## I. Executive Summary
- Introduction
- Summary Tables
- EEMs, WEMs & RCMs Performance Analysis Summary
- EEM, WEM & RCM Financial Analysis Summary
- Next Steps

## II. ASHRAE Level II Energy Audit Report
- Introduction
- Energy Audit Procedures
- EEMs Cost Savings Summary Table
- Existing Conditions and Observations
  - General Building Description
  - Building Envelope
  - HVAC and Control Systems
  - Lighting System & Control
  - Domestic Hot Water
  - Other Equipment
- Energy Efficiency Measures Recommended
  - EEM 1-5 (detailed descriptions)
- EEMs Already Implemented
- EEMs to Consider
- EEMs Requiring Further Investigation
- Current ENERGY STAR® Benchmarking Test
- Energy Use Analysis
  - Aggregated Electricity
  - Aggregated Natural Gas
  - Building Energy End Use Charts
  - Weather Effects on Energy Use
  - Aggregated Electric and Gas Consumption Table

## III. Water Audit Report
- Introduction
- Water Audit Procedures
- WEMs Cost Savings Summary Table
- Existing Conditions and Observations
  - Domestic Indoor Water Use
  - Non-Domestic Indoor Water Use
  - Outdoor Water Use
  - Industrial Process Water use
- Water Efficiency Measures Recommended
  - WEM 1-6 (detailed descriptions)
- WEMs Already Implemented
- WEMs to Consider
- WEMs Requiring Further Investigation
- Non-Efficiency Water Measure/s
- Water Use Analysis
  - Building Water Use Intensity
  - ENERGY STAR® Water Scorecard
  - Water Usage and Cost
  - Water Balance Chart
  - Water Consumption Table

## IV. Retro-commissioning Report
- Introduction
- Objectives
- Overview of Results
- RCMs Cost Savings Summary Table
- Overview of Project Steps
- Retro-commissioning Plan
  - Cooling systems
  - Heating systems
  - Lighting systems & Control
  - EMS/BMS System and Controls
  - Domestic hot water equipment
  - Irrigation system
- Functional Performance Test Methodology
- Assessment Team
- Functional Performance Test Findings & Recommendations
  - RCM 1-4 (detailed descriptions)
- Operational Training

## V. Appendices
- Assumptions
- Equations
- Supporting Documents
  - Equipment List
  - Functional Test Sheets
  - Building drawings
  - Equipment Literature
- Equivalent Full Load Hours
- Additional O&M
- LADWP Rebates
